📲 Share Todo-a
Anyone scanning this QR code opens the app instantly.

📱 iPhone — Add to Home Screen

  1. Open the link in Safari on iPhone
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Confirm — Todo-a appears as an app icon

💻 Mac — Add to Dock

  1. Open the link in Safari
  2. Click the File menu → Add to Dock…
  3. Confirm — Todo-a appears in your dock as a real app

🤖 Android / Chrome

  1. Open the link in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu → Install app (or Add to Home Screen)
  3. Confirm — Todo-a installs as a standalone app
